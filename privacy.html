Privacy Policy for Score & Record - Table Tennis

Provided by Randy H

Effective date: August 2, 2025

Summary

We do not collect, store, or sell personal data. No accounts, no analytics, no ads, no tracking.

Videos are processed on-device and saved to your Photos library only when you choose to export. Temporary files live locally and are periodically deleted.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • Names, emails, phone numbers, or contact lists
  • Precise location data
  • Usage analytics, advertising IDs, or device fingerprinting
  • Health, financial, or other sensitive categories

We do not operate servers that receive your content.

On-Device Processing

All recording and score overlay rendering happens on your device. The app does not upload your media or metadata to us.

Permissions We Request

  • Camera: record video.
  • Microphone: record audio with video.
  • Photos: save exported videos to your library (uses Add-Only where possible).

You can change permissions anytime in Settings → Privacy & Security.

How We Handle Videos & Files

  • Recording: raw recordings are created in local temporary storage.
  • Export: processed videos (with overlays) are saved to Photos when you confirm.
  • Temporary files: used only to complete recording/export and periodically cleaned up by the app.

You control retention: delete items in Photos or uninstall the app to remove local app data.

Children’s Privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information.

Third-Party Sharing & Advertising

  • No third-party advertising SDKs
  • No sale or sharing of data
  • No analytics or tracking tools

Security

We do not store your data on our servers. On-device protections are provided by iOS (passcode, biometrics, sandboxing).

International Data Transfers

We do not transfer your data internationally because we do not collect or store it on servers.

Your Choices & Controls

  • Manage Camera, Microphone, and Photos permissions in iOS Settings.
  • Delete exported videos from the Photos app.
  • Uninstall the app to remove local app data and temporary files.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect improvements or legal requirements. We will update the “Effective date” above and note material changes in-app or on our website.
